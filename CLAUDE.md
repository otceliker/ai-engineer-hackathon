# ai-engineer-hackathon — project instructions

## Maintain the project wiki — `docs/WIKI.md`

`docs/WIKI.md` is the living notebook for this repo. **From now on, whenever you
produce anything worth remembering, append it to the relevant section of
`docs/WIKI.md` in the same turn, before treating the task as done.** That includes:

- every **finding** or result (numbers, benchmarks, accuracies)
- every **table** of results
- every **image / figure / artifact** (save under `docs/assets/`, embed it in the wiki)
- every **design decision** and the reason for it
- every **gotcha / bug / fix** worth not rediscovering
- every **verdict / recommendation**

Bias toward capturing too much — we trim later. Conventions: newest entries at the
bottom of each section, convert relative dates to absolute, link related notes, and
keep `RESULTS.md` (headline numbers) in sync when results change.
